Purpose

Why we built Activate

Problem

Today, personal health has become a major part of every society, and we want to contribute to that greater purpose. Our main goal is to improve the health and fitness of people all around of world, and we acknowledge that individuals are different, and each human being has different physical conditions. Whatever the condition may be, we wanted to assist people in improving or maintaining good physical(or mental) health and fitness.

Solution

Again, everyone is different and everyone has distinct physical conditions. We thought that the best way to solve the problem was to create a program that can take a look at those individual conditions and provide them with certain exercises to complete or certain diets to go on based on some questions they have answered regarding their physical health.

Features

What goes into building a digitalized personal trainer.

Getting Started

Log in screen where the user logs in or signs up with their username and password.

Questionnaire

An all-about-me page where the user is asked a series of questions regarding his/her physical conditions(health, diets, fitness)

Dashboard

Like the user's home page. This is where the user views recommended exercises, diets, and activities.

Profile

Shows the user's statistics while using the app(height, weight, activity level)
